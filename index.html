Why Spark Academy

Purposeful Play. Exceptional Beginnings.

At Spark Academy, play isn't just play — it's the vehicle for everything your child will learn. Every block, every book, every exploration is chosen with intention, designed by a doctoral researcher who has studied what works. This is early education built on evidence, delivered with warmth.

Purposeful Play

Play is the vehicle. Purpose is the engine. Every activity at Spark is chosen because the research says it builds something specific in your child's development.

Kindergarten Confidence

We don't just prepare children for kindergarten — we prepare them to walk in confidently. Academic readiness and social-emotional growth, built together.

Known and Nurtured

Every child is known by name, by temperament, and by their unique way of learning. Small class sizes mean no child is ever invisible.

Expert-Led, Research-Grounded

Spark's curriculum is designed by a doctoral researcher who has studied what works. Every classroom decision has a "why" grounded in evidence.

Community and Belonging

Spark is embedded in Morton — not a franchise, not going anywhere. Families don't just enroll their child — they join a community. Chamber-connected, locally invested, and built for families across the greater Peoria area.

Meet the Founder

Dr. Michelle Peterson, Ed.D.

Educator · Researcher · Educational Leader · Community Builder

Dr. Peterson didn't open a daycare. She built the preschool she wished existed — one grounded in doctoral-level research on how young children actually learn, grow, and develop the confidence to thrive.

With a Doctorate in Education focused on peer engagement and autism, and a background in improvement science and educational leadership, Dr. Peterson brings a level of expertise to the classroom that no corporate chain can replicate. At Spark Academy, the person designing the curriculum is the same person who has researched what works.
