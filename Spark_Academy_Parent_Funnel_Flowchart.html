Stage 4 — Post-Conversion
Thank-You Page (Tour)
/tour-confirmed/
Confirms booking. "What to expect" section. Embedded survey: "What made you reach out?" Links to Dr. Peterson's page or testimonials.
Auto-Confirmation Email
Immediate. Warm greeting from Dr. Peterson. Tour logistics. What to bring. Reply instructions. Keeps Spark in the inbox and top of mind.
Personal Follow-Up
Dr. Peterson or team member confirms date/time. Personal touch = competitive advantage. Builds relationship before the visit.
The Tour Visit
The website's job is done. The school experience takes over. → Enrollment.
