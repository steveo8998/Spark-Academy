General Style Direction

  • Natural light — use window light, avoid harsh flash
  • Warm, homelike tone — the school feels like a home, not an institution
  • Authentic moments over posed shots — candid over staged, always
  • Children are the protagonists — teachers support, not center-stage
  • Teachers at child-level — kneeling, sitting, engaged
  • Diversity in children where possible
  • No identifiable branding from other companies on clothing or materials
  • Warm color temperature — soft teals, golds, sage greens, cream

File Delivery Specs

Format High-res JPEG or TIFF (min 300 DPI)
Resolution 2400px+ for heroes; 1200px+ for all others
Color space sRGB (for web)
Naming Descriptive (e.g., hero-children-academic-play.jpg)
Delivery Edited photos + raw originals

Photo Consent & Releases

All children photographed must have a signed photo release on file with Spark Academy before the shoot. Dr. Peterson to confirm which children have existing releases and coordinate with families. Any parents or staff appearing in photos must also confirm consent.
